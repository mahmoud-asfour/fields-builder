/ About Us
With its Türkiye-based production, multinational identity, and 13 brands, Integral Spor currently delivers its products and services to customers in more than 90 countries.

Fields Builder was founded with a visionary spirit to set new standards in sports facility construction. Over two decades, we have evolved from a local manufacturer into a global leader, dedicated to providing high-performance turf and turnkey solutions for professional and community sports venues.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every pitch we build, whether it's a FIFA-certified stadium or a local community court. We believe that every athlete deserves a field that inspires greatness.
