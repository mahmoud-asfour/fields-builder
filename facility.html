Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are FIFA's requirements for a standard stadium?

The requirements set by FIFA include that the stadium complies with safety standards, that there are stands of the right size and height, that the football field is of appropriate dimensions and that there is an artificial turf surface to be used in international matches.

2. What type of artificial turf is typically used in a 30,000 seats stadium?

For high-capacity stadiums, we recommend our professional-grade artificial turf or hybrid grass solutions that meet FIFA Quality Pro standards, ensuring durability and optimal ball performance under heavy use.

3. What are the general dimensions of a 30,000 seats stadium?

A typical 30,000-seat stadium occupies approximately 30,000 to 45,000 square meters, depending on the architectural design, integrated facilities, and safety perimeter requirements.
