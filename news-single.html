Preparing for the Olympics with Olympic Sports City!

With its Türkiye-based production, multinational identity, and 13 brands, Fields Builder currently delivers its products and services to customers in more than 90 countries. Fields Builder, which works like a heavy industry factory and also creates special social areas, adds a visionary spirit to every project with the concept of Olympic Sports City.

Why Olympic Sports City?

Olympic Sports City is not just a collection of sports facilities; it is a meticulously planned urban ecosystem designed to foster athletic excellence and community engagement. By integrating state-of-the-art infrastructure with world-class management, Fields Builder creates an environment where athletes can thrive at every level.

What Does Olympic Sports City Include?

Fully Equipped Sports Facilities & International Standards:

  • Olympic stadiums designed for major international events,
  • Professional-grade training centers for elite athletes,
  • Modern indoor sports halls for multi-disciplinary training,
  • Comprehensive medical and recovery facilities for sports science.

Accommodation, Rest Areas & Complete Complex Experience

We understand that peak performance requires optimal rest. Fields Builder's sports city concept includes premium athlete villages, integrated hospitality zones, and lush green social areas to ensure a balanced lifestyle for everyone within the complex.

The Advantages & Competitive Strength of Olympic Sports City

Fields Builder's Olympic Sports City stands out due to its modular design, allowing for future expansion and adaptability to different sports and event requirements. Our commitment to using sustainable materials and energy-efficient building techniques ensures that these facilities remain eco-friendly and cost-effective in the long run.
